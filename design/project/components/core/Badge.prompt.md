Status pill for connection state, campaign status, tracker sessions.

```jsx
<Badge tone="positive" dot>Connected</Badge>
<Badge tone="warning" dot>Pending</Badge>
<Badge tone="error" dot>Disconnected</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral">Draft</Badge>
```

`positive` (Sky Blue) is this system's success color — never green. `warning`=amber, `error`=red are the only two non-blue tones and are reserved for those exact meanings.
